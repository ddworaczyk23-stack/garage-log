About this concept

Coast flips the maintenance app inside out. Instead of a logbook you must keep, it's a triage desk you visit at the anxious moment: a warning light, a new noise, a $900 quote.

Every answer arrives as a verdict on the same four-signal scale — Fix now, Book soon, You can coast, All clear — with a fair price range and a script for the shop counter. History builds itself from receipt photos, so it works for people who will never log an oil change.

Prototype: the “noise → braking → grinding” path is fully wired. Other paths show a note.
